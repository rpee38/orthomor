Phonetic codes

Phonetic codes corresponding to standard computer characters were used to ensure compatibility across platforms. The IPA values corresponding to the computer characters are given below. Note that a hatch mark (#) is also used in grapheme-phoneme mappings to indicate a silent grapheme (e.g., the grapheme "t" at the end of a word when not pronounced, as in the word "fort" / fOR /).
